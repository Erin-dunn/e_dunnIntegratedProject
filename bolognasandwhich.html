The Most Delicious Bologna Sandwhich of all time!

Ready to sink your teeth into the ost delicious thing you could find in your fridge at 3am?

Step One

Get your ingredients

  • Bologna
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Mustard

Step Two

place your bread on your makeshift plate, in my case we used a piece of paper towel, however, plastic containers or that test that you failed last week works too.

Step Three

Next you'll want to apply the butter to each slice of bread

Step Four

Next you'll want to apply the mustard, this flavour isn't everyone's favourite flavour so it can be replaced with ketchup, ranch or whatever sort of conidment you have that will hide the fact that you're eating bologna.

Step Five

Add the bologna. You decide how many slices you want to torture yourself with, I chose to use simply 2 slices.

DONE

And thats all there is to it. You're sandwhich is done and you can now get back to that heinous coding project you've been putting off for weeks!
