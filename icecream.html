Making your own Ice Cream

This recipe is a bit more advanced than the others so if you want a beginner recipe, check out something else before coming to this one

Step One

Get your ingredients

  • Salt
  • Milk & Heavy Cream
  • Plastic bags
  • Ice
  • Vanilla
  • Toppings

Step Two

Combine the milk and cream, approximately 2 cups of milk and 1 cup of cream, then combine the vanilla about 1 teaspoon(you can add more if you want it to be sweeter)

Step Three

Next step is to take you mixed ingredients and put them into a plastic sealable bag.

Step Four

Take Some Ice and place it into a seperate plastic sealable bag

Step Five

Add Salt to the bag that has ice in it

Step Six

Take the bag that has the milk, cream and vanilla then place it into the bag with the salt and ice, making sure not to let the bag spill.

Step Seven

Once everythin is sealed up, simply shake the bag for about 15 to 20 minutes and at the end you'll have delicious icecream ready for whatever you want to top it with
