Making Popcorn

It's 2am and the post study mucnhies are kicking in, what do you want to snack on? Chips? Chocolate? NO. Popcorn. It's the perfect snack, the lightness, the salitiness, the convienence. What could go wrong? Ah yes, of course, you don't have any bags of popcorn left. You're mom packed you some popcorn kernels but do you know what to do with them? Well now you do.

Step One

Get all your ingredients

  • Microwavable Bowl
  • Saran Wrap
  • Popcorn Kernels
  • Butter(optional)

Step Two

place your popcorn kernels into the microwavable bowl

Step Three

In the next step, take the saran wrap and place it over the bowl, covering the entire top.

Step Four

Next up take your bowl and place it in the microwave aka your bestfriend, for approximately 2 minutes

DONE

Once you hear your microwave beep, you will be blessed with perfectly popped popcorn. You can then apply your toppings as wanted
